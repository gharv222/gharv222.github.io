Personal Life

Aside from his owner and the other humans in Lucic's life, he maintains a close relationship with a Golden Retriever named Walker. Walker and Lucic have been friends ever since Lucic moved to Manchester. There are times however, where they don't get along, mainly due to Lucic always wanting to play a little to rough. Although walker is Lucic's only dog friend they consider each other best friends.
