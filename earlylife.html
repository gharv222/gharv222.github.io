Early Life

(Lucic as a Puppy)

A First Start

Lucic was born on March 12th, 2016 at the Briar Brook Kennel in Pittsfield New Hampshire. Lucic parent's, Sarge and Penny, were both native's of Pittsfield and had 12 other pups together. At eight weeks old Lucic was adopted by John Doe and moved to Manchester New Hampshire were he resides today. Since his adoption Lucic has almost fully grown, made new friends,learned several tricks, and has grown to love his home in Manchester.
